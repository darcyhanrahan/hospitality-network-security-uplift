# Lessons Learned

## Small Business Networks Often Lack Documentation

Many small organisations operate without accurate network documentation.

Creating a device inventory and architecture diagram significantly improves maintainability.

---

## Default Credentials Are Still Extremely Common

Even in operational environments, factory credentials are frequently left unchanged.

Credential management remains a critical first step in security hardening.

---

## Incremental Security Improvements Provide Major Benefit

Many vulnerabilities identified during the assessment were straightforward to remediate.

Simple actions such as credential rotation, firmware updates, and secure remote access significantly improved overall security posture.

---

## Security Improvements Must Not Disrupt Business Operations

All remediation work was planned to minimise impact to operational systems such as POS terminals and surveillance equipment.

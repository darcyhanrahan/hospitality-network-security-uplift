# Firmware and Device Hardening

## Problem

Several infrastructure devices were running outdated firmware versions.

Outdated firmware may contain known vulnerabilities or stability issues.

---

## Remediation

The following actions were taken:

* Updated infrastructure devices to supported firmware versions
* Verified system stability after updates
* Reviewed device configuration settings for unnecessary services

---

## Outcome

Updating firmware reduced exposure to known vulnerabilities and improved overall device stability.
